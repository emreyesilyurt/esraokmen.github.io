Texture Mapping

Efficient UV unwrapping and texture mapping are essential skills in my 3D asset creation workflow. I create optimized UVs with minimal stretching and efficient texel density to ensure high-quality textures while maintaining performance in-game.

Hand Painting

Hand-painting in 3D modeling is when I manually paint textures onto a model, adding colors, shadows, and highlights to create a stylized, vibrant look.

Trim Sheets

Trim sheets in 3D art are texture maps designed to be reused across multiple models, allowing me to add detailed surfaces (like panels, edges, or patterns) efficiently. Instead of unique textures for each object, I create a single strip-based texture and map different parts of it onto various assets.
